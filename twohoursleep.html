Two Hours Sleep

Designed as Freelancer

Description

Two Hours Sleep is a media company based in the UK passionate about development and always staying on the cutting edge of technology. Their love for their craft has led them to create a brand as a dedicated startup that works around the clock. Based on this, Two Hours Sleep wanted a friendly, illustrative heavy redesign that emulated their brand.

UX/UI Design

From a design perspective, I wanted to incorporate the idea of "sleep", which led to utilizing darker colors and wave-like line illustrations to portray sleep waves. Using animation, the concept was dramatized with scrolling transitions from dark/light opacity giving visitors the emotional impact that there is a passage of time.
